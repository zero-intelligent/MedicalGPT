Summary总结
Diabetes is a complex syndrome requiring
疾病，需要持续的，终身的治疗。
Symptoms of diabetes may result in significant
compromise to quality of life, and even death糖
尿病的症状可能会极大地影响生活，甚至威胁生命。
Owner compliance and financial commitment
must be excellent in order to assure quality of
life is maintained.主人在照顾和财力上的支持才能确保动物的生
