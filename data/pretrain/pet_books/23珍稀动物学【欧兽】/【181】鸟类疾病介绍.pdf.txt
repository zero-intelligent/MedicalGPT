Birds are able to withstand comparatively greater blood loss than mammals
与哺乳动物相比，鸟类能够耐受更大的失血量
o increased capillary surface area within skeletal muscle for rapid extravascular
fluid resorption to maintain vascular volume
。增加骨骼肌内毛细血管表面积以加快循环外体液的重吸收来维持血容量

·能够动员大量的未成熟红细胞
· absence of autonomic response to haemorrhage → haemorrhagic shock
